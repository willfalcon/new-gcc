What We Believe

Grace Community Church is a group of Christ followers scattered throughout the Jackson-Metro area who: exist for Jesus (Colossians 1:16), have been purchased by Jesus (1 Corinthians 6:20), and are seeking to magnify the worth and beauty of Jesus in everything we do (Phillippians 1:20-21). Simply stated, “CHRIST IS ALL”, to us (Colossians 3:11)
